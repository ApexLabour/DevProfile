About us

We are one of South Africa's leading laser cutting service providers. Our unmatched reputation for steel profiling ensures that we are recognized as the most trusted name within the industries that we support and service.

With our cutting edge equipment and highly skilled team we deliver precission steel processing solutions to industries such as engineering, mining, fabrication, and many more.

Dev Profile Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a 100% Black Owned Company & a level 1 B-BBEE contributor based in Apex, Benoni, Gauteng. The company was founded in 2019 by Mr. Devan Naidoo.

  • At Dev Profile Solutions (DPS) we offer our customers a one-stop turnkey solution. We pride ourselves in creating long-term partnerships.
  • Our focus is on streamlining your project with our project management skills through our agility, comprehensive product traceability and pro-active solution-seeking mindset from inception to completion.
  • At DPS we employ only the latest technologies and systems, ensuring that our capabilities extend far beyond industry expectations.

At Dev Profile Solutions we stop at nothing to deliver flawless, unrivalled results across the services that we specialise in, including: Fiber Laser Cutting, Chamfering, Flame Cutting, Drilling, and more.

CAD Software Capabilities

At DPS we have intergrated our full suite of capabilities into our in-house Computer Aided Design (CAD) software, to ensure precision, quality and quick processing.

Some of the benefits of using CAD software:

  • Precise Measurements: Crucial for creating intricate designs to exact specifications.
  • Reduced Errors: Minimizes the risk of human error ensuring designs are accurate.
  • Improved Design Quality: Quality designs result in better products and structures.
  • Faster Design Cycles: Allows designers to create and modify designs efficiently.

Having our CAD team under the same roof and working with the latest design software technologies enables us to deliver optimum accuracy and precision, fasterproductivity and superior quality outcomes, as expected by our customers.

Our CAD team has the expertise to work with clients to ensure we tailor every solution we undertake to our clients' specifications.

1000 m² Factory Premises

Dev Profile Services (DPS) has a 1000 m² factory premises, equipped with a 5-ton overhead gantry crane situated in the heart of Apex, Benoni, Gauteng.

Our premises is conveniently located on a power grid which is totally uneffected by load-shedding and unexpected power outages.

We do, however, have a backup 500Kva generator at our disposal to ensure full production capacity.

  • 1000 m² factory premises with 5-ton overhead gantry crane.
  • Located on a grid unaffected by load-shedding.
  • Backup 500Kva generator ensures full produvtion capacity.

Our client's can rest assured that we make every effort to ensure that our clients' projects are completed on time and to the exacting specifications we're used to providing.

State of the Art Machinery and Equipment

At DPS we use the latest, state of the art fiber laser cutting, flame cutting, chamfering, and drilling machinery and equipment to ensure accurate and quick turnaround times for our clients' projects.

Designed more for production runs, our machinery allows us to load and offload material while the machine is in progress, saving precious time. With the full protection covers, fume extraction is maximised allowing for better working conditions.

At our disposal we have:

  • A 6000 Watt Laser Cutting machine with a 3 meter bed.
  • The ability to cut solid steel up to 16mm in thickness.
  • A Flame Cutter with a 6 meter bed, capable of cutting up to 150mm in thickness.

You can feel assured that at DPS we have the right tools for the job and a highly skilled team behind them to get every job done right, the first time and on time.
